h1>How to Book a Train Ticket

Booking a train ticket is a simple process. Follow the steps given below to book your train ticket easily.

Steps to Book a Train Ticket

  1. Visit the official railway website or open a railway booking app.
  2. Create an account or log in using your username and password.
  3. Enter journey details such as:
    • From Station
    • To Station
    • Journey Date
    • Travel Class
  4. Click on the Search Trains button.
  5. Select a suitable train from the available list.
  6. Check seat availability and choose your preferred class.
  7. Enter passenger details like name, age, and gender.
  8. Select payment method and complete the payment.
  9. After successful payment, your ticket will be confirmed.

Important Points to Remember

  • Carry a valid ID proof during travel.
  • Check your ticket status before boarding the train.
  • Keep a digital or printed copy of your ticket.

Happy Journey!
